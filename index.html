About DF Studios

Built for
Collectors.

At DF Studios, we believe your LEGO builds deserve far more than a shelf. Our custom wall-mounted display frames transform completed sets into clean, modern wall art — protecting every detail of your craftsmanship while putting it on proud display.

Based in Sheffield Beach, KwaZulu-Natal, every frame is crafted using premium materials selected to complement your LEGO set. From the aerodynamic curves of an F1 Speed Champions car to the mechanical complexity of a Technic build, we make sure your frame enhances — not competes with — your model.

We focus on quality design, precision craftsmanship, and reliable service — because every collector deserves to display their builds with real pride.
